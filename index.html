五十十五二十 (5 10 15 20) - Game Rules

Background and Origin

Instructions:

Objective: Guess the total number of fingers shown by both players with the called number.

  • 1. Play rock-paper-scissors to determine the first caller.
  • 2. The caller shouts one of the following numbers: 0, 5, 10, 15, or 20.
  • 3. Both players must show their hands/fingers at the same time as the caller calls the number. Each player can raise 0 or 5 fingers on one hand.
  • 4. Count the total number of fingers raised by both players.
  • 5. If the total matches the called number, the caller goes again. If not, the turn passes to the other player to call.
  • 6. Additionally, a player can call open (for twenty fingers) and closed (for 0 fingers)
  • 7. If the caller correctly guesses the total number of fingers twice in a row, they win that round.
  • 8. The winner of each round goes up a step the loser goes down a step (unless they are at the bottom).
  • 9. As a penalty, if you lose a round, close one eye; you can reopen both eyes on any win, even if both are closed.

Note: Quick reactions and bluffing can make the game more fun!
