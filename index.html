五十十五二十 (5 10 15 20) - Game Rules

Objective: Guess the total number of fingers correctly to avoid drinking.

Instructions:

  • 1. Players sit in a circle and take turns calling numbers.
  • 2. On a player's turn, they call out, "0", "5", "10", "15", or "20".
  • 3. Everyone must raise 0 or 5 fingers on one hand.
  • 4. If the total fingers match the called number, the caller is safe. If not, they drink.
  • 5. The turn moves to the next player.

Note: Quick reactions and bluffing can make the game more fun!
